##**Hey!**
My name is Csipesz. I made this template to fit my friends’ and my own playstyle, but I thought I’d share it with others too.

We enjoy playing in a way where the first 2–3 months are all about preparation, and then everything is decided in one huge final battle. That’s what the hub is for: it’s the only zone from which you can enter the territories of other players. For us, a single match usually took around 2–3 hours of gameplay.

A short description of the template:
Every player starts with 1 castle and 2 heroes (you need to buy the second one or rescue them).
With every additional castle, the hero limit increases by 1.
Every player has 4 treasuries: 1 low, 1 medium, 1 high, and an another 1 high with abandoned outpost.
The border guards are fairly strong; the recommended difficulty is 125–150%.

I hope you’ll enjoy it! Of course, there are still things that could be tweaked here and there, and I’ll probably update it from time to time.

For anyone who doesn’t know how to install it, here’s a quick guide:

Copy the contents of the package into this folder:
\steamapps\common\Heroes of Might and Magic Olden Era\HeroesOldenEra_Data\StreamingAssets\map_templates
Done.
