# Welcome!

These templates were originally created for my friends and my owm playstyle, but i figured other might enjoy them too.
> Our games are built around a long preparation phase followed by one massive final war.
> Usually the first 2-3 months are spent building up armies, scouting, and planning.
> After that everything is decided in a huge late-game clash.

### Template Features
- Every player starts with:
  + 1 Castle
  + 2 Heroes *(the second hero must be purchased or rescued)*
- Hero limit:
  + Each additional castle increases the hero cap by +1
- Each player has:
  + 1 Low treasury
  + 1 Medium treasury
  + 2 High treasury
    + One of them contains an Abandoned Outpost
- Strong border guards
  + Recommended difficulty:
    + 100% - 150%

### Installation:

- Copy the contents of the package into this folder:
` \steamapps\common\Heroes of Might and Magic Olden Era\HeroesOldenEra_Data\StreamingAssets\map_templates `
- Done.

There's two template available on the [releases](https://github.com/CsipeszRealm/Fighthubtemplates/releases) page


## Notes
These templates were designed mainly for:
- Long macro-focused games
- Delayed PvP
- One decisive final battle
- Competitive matches with friends
If you enjoy slower buildup and explosive endgames, this template might fit your playstyle perfectly
